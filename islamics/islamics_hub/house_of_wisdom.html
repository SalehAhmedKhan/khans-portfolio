The House of Wisdom

This is my ongoing personal project to archive Islamic information, knowledge, history, language, and heritage in one calm, organized space. I am building it as a living collection that can grow over time through notes, timelines, reflections, and carefully structured resources.

If you want to contribute, suggest a correction, or share useful resources — contact me!
